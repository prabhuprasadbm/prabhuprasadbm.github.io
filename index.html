Prabhu Prasad B M

I am currently working as an Assistant Professor at Indian Institute of Information Technoology Dharwad, India. I have a Ph.D from SPARK Lab , Dept. of Computer Science and Engineering, National Institute of Technology Karnataka, Surathkal, under the supervision of Dr. Basavaraj Talawar..

My research focuses on the field of Network-on-Chips, FPGAs, Simulation acceleration, High-performance computing.
